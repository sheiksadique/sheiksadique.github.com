Welcome to my home page !

Nothing can match the happiness and satisfaction derived from knowing that one's innovations and ideas are being applied to advance the society and that they are appreciated by people around the globe. A strong urge to learn and then explain real-life observations on the basis of such learning has enthralled me since my school days.


One of the projects I worked on titled "Excited Damped Double Pendulum" drew my attention to biological sciences, when I tried to correlate the double pendulum problem to the human limbs studying its dynamics. Human body structure was studied in great detail by Leonardo da Vinci; his work "The Vitruvian man" was one of my inspirations to take this path. Leonardo da Vinci is said to be an observational physicist and borrowed many ideas from nature. After the completion of my project I tried experimenting with the variables so as to fit the model with the limbs of Da-Vinci's "perfect man". To my astonishment the problem's complexity was reduced to a great extent and an interesting phenomenon was observed. The motion no longer had multiple modes. This made me wonder about the perfectness in the world, about the innumerous ideas and immense knowledge available in nature.


Human subtlety will never devise an invention more beautiful, more simple or more direct than does Nature, because in her inventions, nothing is lacking and nothing is superfluous. Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication. -Leonardo da Vinci

Here is WHY this page came into existence.

I have been dreaming to have a nice little home page for years now. But every time I got started on developing one, I was only left wanting more from the page. I only ended up trying to learn more and more web technologies and never actually developing the website!


So one fine day when I was tyring (to find an excuse) to do my work and write my thesis, I said to myself "Hmm.. I should build my home page now!". And so here we are!!
