Research Interests

Understanding the principles of nature has always been at the core for the evolution of physics and engineering. The human brain is perhaps the pinnacle of this endeavor. I wish to further our understanding and employ it to design neuromorphic devices to perform robust, energy-efficient cognitive computation.


Spiking Neural Networks
  • Recurrent networks, Learning and Classification of spatio-temporal patterns.
  • Learning rules, pairwise/triplet/calcium based STDP.
  • Multi-modal, sensory integration.

Machine Learning / Deep Learning
  • Understanding cognitive computation from a machine learning perspective.
  • Unsupervised learning, Generative models, Reinforcement Learning.
  • Reinforcement learning.

Neuromorphic Engineering
  • Substrate driven computation.
  • Computational models to exploit peculiarities in neuromorphic engineering (like mismatch).
  • Architecture design for neuromorphic processors.
  • Generic description of neuromorphic chips and standardization.
  • Software interface to neuromorphic chips.
