Here is WHY this page came into existence.

I have been dreaming to have a nice little home page for years now. But every time I got started on developing one, I was only left wanting more from the page. I only ended up trying to learn more and more web technologies and never actually developing the website!


So one fine day when I was tyring (to find an excuse) to do my work and write my thesis, I said to myself "Hmm.. I should build my home page now!". And so here we are!!

Here is HOW this page came into existence.

I figured I wasn't artistic enough to put together a nice page from scratch and neither am I patient enough to do that. So I borrowed from a bunch of template pages from different places and worked my way from there. So here are the sources:

  • Template: An Ocean of Sky by Bryant Smith
  • CV page: http://sampleresumetemplate.net/


And ofcourse the website that has taught me everything I know about web technology is w3schools.com. One of my friends, who is a web expert, once told me that w3schools is not a good place to learn from. But hey as long as I can get my work done, who cares about technicalities!! Anyway I had to leave this disclaimer just in case.
